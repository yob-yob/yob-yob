Progressive Bodyweight Training Plan

120-Day structured progression (reusable beyond 120 days).
Train 3–4 days per week. Rest or light activity between hard sessions.

Phase 1 (Days 1–30) — Grade 4: Foundation

ExerciseSets × Reps / HoldNotes
Straight-arm plank3 × 30–45sCore, shoulders, glutes
Pike hold3 × 20sShoulder overload
Squats3 × 15–20Use box squat if knee issues
Dead hang3 × 20–30sGrip + back
Active hang3 × 20sScapula control
Scapular shrugs2–3 × 8–10Slow and controlled
Hollow body hold3 × 10–20sCore finisher

Plank variations2–3 × 30sCore stability

Phase 4 (Days 91–120) — Grade 1: Advanced

Bodyweight lateral raise2 × 10Shoulder shaping

Lower + Core

ExerciseSets × RepsNotes

L-sit3 × 10–20sCore + hip flexors
Plank variations3 × 30–60sCore stability

Notes

  • Progress when you hit the top rep/hold range with good form.
  • Rest 60–90s between sets, 2–3 min for harder moves (pull-ups, dips, pistols).
  • Deload every 4th week: reduce volume by ~30%.
  • Recommended frequency: 3–4 training days per week.
