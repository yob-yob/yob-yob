Progressive Bodyweight Training Plan

120-Day structured progression (reusable beyond 120 days).
Train 3–4 days per week. Rest or light activity between hard sessions.

Phase 1 (Days 1–30) — Grade 4: Foundation

Exercise Sets × Reps / Hold Notes
Straight-arm plank 3 × 30–45s Core, shoulders, glutes
Pike hold 3 × 20s Shoulder overload
Squats 3 × 15–20 Use box squat if knee issues
Dead hang 3 × 20–30s Grip + back
Active hang 3 × 20s Scapula control
Scapular shrugs 2–3 × 8–10 Slow and controlled
Hollow body hold 3 × 10–20s Core finisher

Phase 2 (Days 31–60) — Grade 3: Ascending Strength

Day A — Push + Core

Exercise Sets × Reps Notes
Incline push-ups 3 × 10–12 Chest strength
Incline pike push-ups 3 × 6–8 Shoulders
Hollow body hold 3 × 20–30s Core tension
Plank variations 2–3 × 30s Core stability

Day B — Pull + Legs

Exercise Sets × Reps Notes
Inverted rows 3 × 8–10 Upper back
Jackknife pull-ups 3 × 6 Assisted pull-up
Lunges 3 × 10 each leg Legs & balance
Scapular shrugs 2 × 8 Shoulder mobility

Phase 3 (Days 61–90) — Grade 2: Intermediate

Push Day

Exercise Sets × Reps Notes
Standard push-ups 4 × 12–15 Chest foundation
Pike push-ups 3 × 8+ Shoulders
Sphinx push-ups 3 × 8–10 Triceps isolation

Pull Day

Exercise Sets × Reps Notes
Inverted rows 4 × 10 Back strength
Pull-up negatives 3 × 5 (5–8s descent) Pull-up strength
Bodyweight curls 3 × 10–12 Biceps isolation
Scapular shrugs 2 × 8 Shoulder mobility

Leg Day

Exercise Sets × Reps Notes
Jump squats 3 × 12–15 Explosive power
Lunges 3 × 12 each leg Unilateral strength
Split squats 3 × 8 each leg Balance + control

Core (Add-on)

Exercise Sets × Reps / Hold Notes
Hollow hold 3 × 20–30s Core foundation
L-sit (tuck ok) 3 × 10–15s Core + hip flexors

Phase 4 (Days 91–120) — Grade 1: Advanced

Upper (Push + Pull)

Exercise Sets × Reps Notes
Dips 3 × 6–8 Push strength
Decline push-ups 3 × 8 Chest + shoulders
Pull-ups / Chin-ups 4 × max reps Back + arms + core
Bodyweight lateral raise 2 × 10 Shoulder shaping
Crow pose / Frog stand 3 × 15–20s Balance + wrists/shoulder

Lower + Core

Exercise Sets × Reps Notes
Pistol squat 3 × 5 each leg Single-leg strength
Jump squats 3 × 12 Explosive legs
Lunges 3 × 10 each leg Balance + strength
L-sit 3 × 10–20s Core + hip flexors
Plank variations 3 × 30–60s Core stability

Notes

  • Progress when you hit the top rep/hold range with good form.
  • Rest 60–90s between sets, 2–3 min for harder moves (pull-ups, dips, pistols).
  • Deload every 4th week: reduce volume by ~30%.
  • Recommended frequency: 3–4 training days per week.
